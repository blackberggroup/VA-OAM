About the VA Office of Advanced Manufacturing

The Office of Advanced Manufacturing (OAM) is revolutionizing care for Veterans by delivering personalized solutions right at the point of care. Whether it's creating assistive devices or designing 3D-printed medical models for surgical planning, OAM's innovative approach ensures Veterans receive care that meets their unique needs. By working closely with clinical teams and using cutting-edge manufacturing technologies, OAM is working to improve the quality of life for Veterans, offering them greater independence and dignity.
